Hobbies

I'm a total technology nerd... If it bleeps, blips, bloops, does computations, runs software, or just basically mind boggles most people; then I'm probably into it. It all started when I hit my teenage years, and I found an obsession in PC's. I found myself ripping apart every piece of electronic equipment I had trying to figure out how it worked, what might be causing issues, or just to try and upgrade it to something better.

I'm also an avid musician and I play guitar, drums, and bass and love most types of music, however, Metal is my favorite. I've most recently been into the new line up of music on Liquid Metal satellite radio. There are some awesome bands hitting the scene, and some old ones making a return, who are worth listening to.

I'm a lover of motorcycles and loud noises as well, one could say those passions probably go hand in hand. I started riding dirt bikes when I was 14 and have preferred the two wheeled method of travel ever since. I currently own a 2019 Harley Davidson Street Bob with Vance and Hinese Short Shots, Roland Sands Turbine intake, and the 107ci Milwaukee 8 engine. You can check out pics on my Instagram linked in the Nav!

Based on my love for computers if you haven't guessed yet, I'm also a lover of video games! I can enjoy pretty much any game genre but RPGs, Shooters, and Action games are among my favorites. I've also been known to get into some horror, racing, and sports games as well. The Fallout and Elder Scross series are probably my favorite games with Cyberpunk 2077 likely to take the top of that list soon!
